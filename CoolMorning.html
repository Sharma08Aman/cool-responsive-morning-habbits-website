Lets start with a good wakeup . . .

There are many perks to waking up early in the morning. Everyone knows that, however to deal with morning sleepyhead feels like a chore tough enough. Here are few ways you can jog your brain early morning.

1.Non-dominant hand brushing

Brushing your teeth with non dominant hand i.e. left hand for a righty person and vice versa proves to be a difficult task for your brain. So task as easy as brushing your teeth with other hand will tingle your brain cells enough to be less sleepyhead and provides stimulation for it, improving creative capablities from the other side of brain at the same time.

2.Black Coffee

Coffee is an effective means of jolting your brain to a rush of energy. However, milk produces lactic acid within our body that induces sleep so drinking a goodnight milk is a great habbit. But you can sense the dillema now of adding milk to your coffee for early morning. It reduces the jolt of coffee. Thus, about 100ml to 150ml of early morning black coffee can wake you up instantly.

3.An hour of any book of your choice

Reading a book of your choice will do the work for you. The effect just not includes giving you good habbit of reading and addition to your knowledge but in morning it also simulates your brain function exponentially for the day.

4.Little bit of cardio

A little bit of exercise in early morning is always good for body. Even 10 mintutes of exercise is enough to jog not just your muscles of entire body but your brain mass too. And an added bonus of improving physique, personality and attitude.
